Introduction

Welcome to the Masaka Social Centre, a vibrant hub dedicated to providing a wide range of services and activities for the community of Masaka City. Our mission is to foster a supportive and engaging environment where individuals of all ages can come together, learn, and grow.

Services Offered

  • Community Workshops and Skill Development Programs
  • Youth and Women Empowerment Initiatives
  • Health and Wellness Programs
  • Educational Support and Tutoring
  • Sports and Recreational Activities
  • Counseling and Guidance Services

More About Us

Established in the heart of Masaka City, Masaka Social Centre is a non-profit organization that caters to the social, educational, and recreational needs of the community. We offer programs that aim to uplift, empower, and connect people, creating a stronger, more united community.
